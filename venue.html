Venue

Conference Venue

Nanyang Executive Centre, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore


60 Nanyang View, Singapore 639673
Telephone: +65 6790 6699/ 6790 6697
Fax number: +65 6794 7860

Changi International Airport to NEC via Taxi

  • Taxis are readily available at the taxi stands of the Arrival Hall of each Terminal (follow the taxi signs at the Arrival Hall).
  • Address: 60 Nanyang View, Singapore 639673
  • Fares are metered and there isn’t a need to pay tips
  • There are additional Airport surcharges for all trips originating from the Airport :
    • SGD$5 Airport surcharge: 5.00pm - midnight (Fri - Sun)
    • SGD$3 Airport surcharge: All other times
Directions:
  • Take the PIE (Pan-Island Expressway) towards Tuas
  • Exit Expressway at Pioneer Road North (Exit 38) and turn right into NTU
  • At round-about, take the 3rd exit
  • Turn left into carpark
  • Approach T-junction and make a right turn
  • NEC is on the left immediately after the right turn
  • Journey time: about 1 hour (during peak period), 45 mins (during off-peak)

Changi International Airport to NEC via MRT (Mass Rapid Transit)

  • The train station is accessible by foot from Terminal 2 or Terminal 3 (Follow the sign at arrival hall).
  • All the trains and gantries at the stations are luggage-friendly.
Directions:
  • Proceed to the vending machine and purchase a standard ticket. Choose the destination to be Boon Lay (EW27). Price: Approximately SGD$2.05
  • Board the train and transit at Tanah Merah Station to East-West line (Green line in the direction towards Joo Koon Station)
  • Alight at Boon Lay Station and take a taxi to Nanyang Executive Centre (NEC)
  • The taxi might take a different route from that described above
  • Journey time: about 2 hours in total
