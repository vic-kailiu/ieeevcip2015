Paper Submission

Camera Ready Instructions 

  1. Authors are to revise and enhance their accepted paper by taking into account the area chair and reviewers’ comments, include their names and affiliations in the paper, and ensure that the camera-ready submission strictly follows the format specifications outlined and illustrated in these sample files: PDF, Word or LaTeX.

  2. To be published in the VCIP 2015 Proceedings and be included in IEEE Xplore® Digital Library, all camera-ready papers must be in PDF format and be compliant with IEEE Xplore®. To validate whether or not your camera-ready paper is IEEE Xplore® compliant, you may visit the IEEE PDF eXpress plus website and follow these steps:

    a) Go to the IEEE PDF eXpress plus website at http://www.pdf-express.org/plus/
    b) Use this conference ID: 36088XP
    c) Use PDF eXpress plus to check your paper and generate a compliant PDF file.

  3. The name of your camera-ready paper must be in the form of XXX.pdf, where XXX is the three-digit paper ID (zero-padded if necessary). For example, if your paper ID is 88, the filename should be 088.pdf. Rename accordingly your camera-ready PDF file that has been validated by PDF eXpress plus.

  4. Your camera-ready paper must be submitted in PDF format and no later than 25 September 2015 (Pacific Daylight Time, PDT) to the VCIP 2015 paper submission website at CMT. To do so, please click on the Camera-Ready link in the “Author Console” after you login to your account at CMT.   Remember to update/modify (if necessary) the paper title, abstract, and author list to match those in your camera-ready paper. Please check that all author names are spelled correctly and are in the correct order.

  5. One author of each accepted paper must submit an Electronic Copyright Form (eCF) to IEEE no later than 30 September 2015 (PDT). To submit your eCF, please click on the IEEE Copyright File link in your “Author Console” at the CMT website. You will be redirected to the IEEE eCF site to submit the copyright form (The eCF cite should be ready to receive submission of Electronic Copyright Forms around 20 September). After filling out the IEEE copyright form on the eCF site, you need to download the form and upload it to the paper submission website at CMT. Papers without copyright forms will not be published in the conference proceedings and will not be included IEEE Xplore® Digital Library.

  6. To be included in the VCIP 2015 program, every accepted paper must be covered by a full-rate registration from one of the authors on or before 30 September 2015.  One full-rate registration can cover at most two accepted papers of the registrant.  Each additional paper requires an extra payment of SGD 400. The paper must be presented by one of the authors (or a qualified substitute) at the conference. Please refer to the conference registration page for details.

Important Notes

Checking your PDF file for compliance at the IEEE PDF eXpress plus website and submitting your camera-ready paper at the CMT website are two separate steps. After using the IEEE PDF eXpress plus website to validate your camera-ready paper and generate a compliant PDF file, MAKE SURE to return to the VCIP 2015 paper submission website at CMT and upload the compliant PDF file as the camera-ready paper.  The compliant file from IEEE PDF eXpress plus is labeled within its document properties as being 'Certified by IEEE PDF eXpress', with an exact date and time stamp.
Important Reminder

To be published in the VCIP 2015 conference proceedings and be included in IEEE Xplore® Digital Library,  each accepted paper must meet all the following conditions:

    • The Camera-Ready Paper strictly follows all the paper format specifications, has been validated by IEEE PDF eXpress plus, and has been uploaded to the VCIP 2015 paper submission website at CMT by 25 September 2015 (PDT).
    • The IEEE Electronic Copyright form is completed online at the IEEE eCF site and then submitted to the VCIP 2015 paper submission website at CMT by 30 September 2015 (PDT).

    • The paper is covered by a full-rate registration of one of the authors by 30 September 2015 (PDT). The paper must also be presented by one of the authors (or a qualified substitute) at the conference.
